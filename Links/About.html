Our Mission: A PlayStation in Every African Home

At Amiimu Gadgets, we believe that gaming is more than just entertainment. It is a gateway to creativity, social connection, and endless possibilities. Our mission is to make premium gaming accessible to every household across Africa, starting right here in Kampala, Uganda.

We understand the passion that drives gamers. That is why we dedicate ourselves to providing authentic, high-quality gaming products at competitive prices. Whether you are a seasoned pro or just starting your gaming journey, we have the right console, accessories, and games for you.

Founded with a vision to transform the gaming landscape in Uganda, Amiimu Gadgets has grown into a trusted name for PlayStation and Xbox products, repairs, and services. We take pride in being more than just a store. We are a community hub for gamers, a service center for consoles, and a reliable partner in your gaming adventure.

Our team of experienced technicians and gaming enthusiasts are committed to ensuring that every product we sell meets the highest standards of quality. From brand new consoles to carefully inspected pre-owned units, every item that leaves our store is backed by our commitment to excellence.
